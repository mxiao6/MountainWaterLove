Feeling from Mountain and Water
---

Principle Scenes:
1, the master waits for boat by the river

2, master on board, noticing the boy's music

3, master faints after debarkation

4, master plays music (琴を弾く) on the bed while the boy enters the house

5, master teaches the boy to play music

6-9, the boy plays music in spring, summer, fall, and winter (with special effects in each)

10, eagles in the sky

11, the boy escorts the master to the mountain

12, master gifts the boy his instrument (琴)

13, the boy plays music on top of the mountain
